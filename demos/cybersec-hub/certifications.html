Guidance

How to Choose the Right Cert

🎯

Consider Your Goal

SOC/Blue team → CySA+, GSOC, GCIH. Penetration testing → eJPT, OSCP, GPEN. Management → CISSP, CISM. Cloud → AWS/Azure Security. Forensics → GCFE, GCFA. Match the cert to your target role.

💼

Check Job Postings

Search LinkedIn/Indeed for your target role. See which certifications appear most frequently. Security+ and CISSP appear in the widest range of job postings. OSCP dominates pentesting ads.

💰

Budget Realistically

SANS/GIAC certs are $5,000–$8,000 with training. CompTIA is $300–$500. OffSec is ~$1,500. Many employers pay for certs — ask during negotiation. Free alternatives exist (ISC² CC, Google Cert).
