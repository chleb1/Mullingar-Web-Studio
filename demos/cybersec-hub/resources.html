Reading List

Essential Books

📗
Beginner
CompTIA Security+ Study Guide
Mike Chapple & David Seidl. The standard Security+ study guide — comprehensive, well-organised, with excellent practice questions. Covers all exam domains.
📗
Beginner
Cybersecurity for Beginners
Raef Meeuwisse. Plain English introduction to cybersecurity — threats, defences, career paths, and the broader ecosystem. Perfect first book for the field.
📗
Beginner
The Art of Invisibility
Kevin Mitnick. How hackers think and how to protect your privacy and digital identity. Accessible storytelling from the world's most famous reformed hacker.
📗
Beginner
Countdown to Zero Day
Kim Zetter. The definitive account of Stuxnet — the world's first known cyberweapon. Reads like a thriller but teaches real concepts. Excellent for motivation and context.
📙
Intermediate
The Web Application Hacker's Handbook
Stuttard & Pinto. Comprehensive web app security bible. Covers every major vulnerability class with technical depth. A must-read before OSCP or BSCP.
📙
Intermediate
Penetration Testing
Georgia Weidman (No Starch Press). Hands-on introduction to penetration testing — network scanning, exploitation, post-exploitation, and reporting.
📙
Intermediate
The Practice of Network Security Monitoring
Richard Bejtlich. Definitive NSM guide — collecting, detecting, and analysing network traffic. The blue team counterpart to any pentesting book.
📙
Intermediate
Hacking: The Art of Exploitation
Jon Erickson. Learn hacking from a programmer's perspective — C, memory, shellcode, and cryptography. Dense but transformative for understanding how systems break.
📕
Pro
Practical Malware Analysis
Sikorski & Honig. The definitive malware analysis textbook. Static analysis, dynamic analysis, anti-analysis techniques, and full lab walkthroughs.
📕
Pro
The Art of Memory Forensics
Ligh et al. Everything about memory forensics — Volatility framework, OS internals, rootkit detection, and malware artefact recovery from RAM.
📕
Pro
Red Team Development and Operations
Joe Vest & James Tubberville. The guide to building and running enterprise red team programs. Programme management, methodology, and reporting.
📕
Pro
The Shellcoder's Handbook
Covering vulnerability discovery and exploitation across platforms. Advanced reading for exploit developers and security researchers.
