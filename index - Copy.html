FAQ

Common questions

I'm not technical at all — is that a problem?

Not in the slightest. Most of our clients have never had a website before. We handle the technical bits (domain, hosting, email setup) and explain everything in plain English. You just need to tell us about your business.

Where are you based? Do you work with businesses nationwide?

We're based in Mullingar but work with businesses all around Ireland. Most projects are handled over email and short video calls — wherever you are, we can make it work. If you're local, an in-person meeting is always an option.

You're students — can I really trust you with this?

Fair question. Between us we have 4+ years of programming experience, we keep prices honest, and we take no deposit until you've approved the design. You can see a live preview before committing. If you're not happy with the preview, you owe us nothing.

What does it actually cost to keep a website online?

Hosting and a domain name usually run between €5 and €15 a month, paid directly to the provider (we don't mark this up). We'll recommend a sensible setup based on your needs and help you sign up.

Can you take payments or bookings on my site?

Yes — we add bookings (via Calendly or SimplyBook) and payments (via Stripe) as add-ons. For most small businesses these aren't needed on day one, but they're easy to add later.

How long does it take?

Once we agree on what to build, most sites are live within 1–2 weeks. Refreshes are often faster. The main thing that slows projects down is waiting for photos or text from the client — we'll help with both if you need.

What about ongoing support?

Every package includes 30 days of free tweaks after launch. After that, small changes are usually €15–€30 each, or you can arrange a flat monthly maintenance fee. No lock-in contracts.
